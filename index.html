About Our Company

Royal Honey, a two-year-old company founded by Mohd Kamran Malik, is nestled in the heart of Pilibhit, Uttar Pradesh. The company’s core mission is to deliver organic honey to every Indian household. They specialize in providing a variety of honey types, all packed with nature’s goodness and purity. Their offerings range from Eucalyptus to Lychee honey, each available in beautifully crafted gift packs. These packs serve as a perfect token of love and health for your dear ones. Royal Honey’s commitment to organic produce ensures that every spoonful of their honey is as pure and beneficial as nature intended. Their journey, although only two years long, is marked by a passion for quality and a promise of authenticity.
